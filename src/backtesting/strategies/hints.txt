| Strategy           | Ideal Timeframes  | Avoid                      |
|--------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------|
| SMA/EMA Cross      | 4h, 1D            | 1m, 5m (too much noise)     |
| RSI                | 15m, 1h, 1D       | 1m, 5m                      |
| MACD               | 1h, 4h, 1D        | 1m, 5m (delayed signals)    |
| Bollinger Bands    | 15m, 1h           | 1m, 1D                      |
| Breakout Strategy  | 1h, 4h            | 1m without confirmation     |
